# Delete Account – Albu-me

If you would like to delete your Albu-me account, please follow the steps below:

1. Open the Albu-me mobile application.
2. Go to Profile → Account Settings.
3. Tap "Delete Account".
4. Confirm deletion.

If you cannot access the app, please send a request to:

contact@albu-me.com

Include the email or phone number associated with your account.

We will permanently delete your account and associated personal data within 7 business days.

Note:
- Order history may be retained if required for legal or accounting purposes.
